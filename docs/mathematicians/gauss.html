Carl Friedrich Gauss

Name: Carl Friedrich Gauss

Nationality: German

Major Contributions:

  • Number theory
  • Differential geometry
  • Statistics
  • Analysis
  • Astronomy

Biography:

Known as the 'Prince of Mathematics', Gauss made significant contributions to many fields including number theory, statistics, analysis, differential geometry, geodesy, geophysics, mechanics, electrostatics, astronomy, matrix theory, and optics.
