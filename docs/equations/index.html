Quadratic Formula

Name: Quadratic Formula

Equation:
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}

Category: Algebra

Description:

The quadratic formula provides the solutions to any quadratic equation of the form $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$.

Applications:

  • Solving quadratic equations
  • Finding roots of parabolas
  • Optimization problems
  • Physics projectile motion

Pythagorean Theorem

Name: Pythagorean Theorem

Equation:
a^2 + b^2 = c^2

Category: Geometry

Description:

In a right triangle, the square of the hypotenuse equals the sum of squares of the other two sides.

Applications:

  • Right triangle calculations
  • Distance calculations
  • Engineering and construction
  • Navigation and GPS systems

Author: pythagoras

Euler's Identity

Name: Euler's Identity

Equation:

Category: Algebra

Often called the most beautiful equation in mathematics, connecting five fundamental mathematical constants (see [e2008]).
