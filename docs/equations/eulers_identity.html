Euler's Identity

Name: Euler's Identity

Equation:
e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0

Category: complex_analysis

Description:

Often called the most beautiful equation in mathematics, connecting five fundamental mathematical constants.

Applications:

  • Complex analysis
  • Signal processing
  • Quantum mechanics
  • Electrical engineering

Author: Leonhard Euler
